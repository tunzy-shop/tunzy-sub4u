Frequently Asked Questions
How do I buy data? ▼
Click on Data button, select network, choose plan, and confirm payment. Data is delivered instantly.
How long do transfers take? ▼
Transfers are usually instant. Maximum 5 minutes during peak periods.
How do I set transaction PIN? ▼
Go to Profile → Security Settings → Toggle Transaction PIN, then set your 4-digit PIN.
